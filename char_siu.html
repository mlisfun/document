Char Siu (叉燒)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Pork Shoulder Butt (磅 上肩肉/梅頭肉)
  • 2 table spoons of Honey (湯匙蜂蜜)
  • 2 table spoons of water (湯匙水)
  • 1 cube of redbean curb (塊紅腐乳)
  • 1 table spoons of oyster source (湯匙蠔油)
  • 1 table spoons of soy source (湯匙醬油)
  • 1 table spoons of dark source (湯匙老抽)
  • 1 table spoons of sake (湯匙清酒)
  • 4 table spoons of sugar (湯匙糖)
  • 1/4 tea spoons of five spice power(茶匙五香粉)
  • 1/4 tea spoons of white pepper powder (茶匙白胡椒)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (瓣蒜)

Preparing Instructions

  • Mince garlic
  • Pour sauce into a bowl and mix the sources
  • Cut pork into 1 inch thickness
  • Use fork to stab the pork with holes
  • Marinate meat with the source.
  • Store marinated meat in fridge for 1 or 2 days.

Cooking Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 425 F
  • Put the meat in the oven for 25 minutes.
  • take it out, flip and brush source.
  • Put the meat in the oven for 25 minutes.
  • Mix 2湯匙蜂蜜 and 少許水
  • take the meat out and brush honey source.
  • Put the meat in for 5 minutes.
