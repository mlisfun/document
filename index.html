Char Siu (叉燒)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Pork Shoulder Butt (磅 上肩肉/梅頭肉)
  • 2 teaspone of Honey (湯匙蜂蜜)
  • 2 teaspoons of water (湯匙水)
  • 2 cubes of redbean curb (塊紅腐乳)
  • 1 tespoons of oyster source (湯匙蠔油)
  • 1 teaspoons of soy source (湯匙醬油)
  • 1 teaspoons of dark source (湯匙老抽)
  • 1 teaspoons of sake (湯匙清酒)
  • 4 teaspoons of sugar (湯匙糖)
  • 1/4 teaspoons of five spice power(湯匙五香粉)
  • 1/4 teaspoons of white pepper powder (湯匙白胡椒)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (瓣蒜)

Preparing Instructions

  • Mince garlic
  • Pour sources into a bowl and mix the sources
  • marinate meat with the source.

Cooking Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 400 F
  • Put the meat in the oven for 25 minutes.
  • take it out, flip and brush source.
  • Put the meat in the oven for 25 minutes.
  • Mix 2湯匙蜂蜜 and 少許水
  • take the meat out and brush honey source.
  • Put the meat in for 5 minutes.
